1. Problem Statement

Patients who undergo major surgical procedures often face several challenges during the recovery period after being discharged from the hospital. The first few weeks after surgery are critical because patients may develop complications such as infections, nutritional deficiencies, dehydration, delayed healing, or other health issues. However, most patients receive only basic discharge instructions and limited follow-up monitoring.

Another major challenge is that medical documents such as discharge summaries, laboratory reports, prescriptions, and radiology reports contain complex medical terminology that is difficult for patients to understand. As a result, many patients fail to recognize important medical information or warning signs in their reports.

Due to the lack of continuous monitoring and proper understanding of medical records, complications may go unnoticed until they become serious, leading to hospital readmissions and delayed treatment. Therefore, there is a need for a system that can assist patients in understanding their medical documents and continuously monitor their recovery progress.

The proposed system aims to address this problem by providing an intelligent application that analyzes medical documents, tracks patient recovery indicators, and offers clear insights and guidance to support safer post-surgery recovery.

2. Objectives of the Project

The main objectives of the proposed system are:

To simplify medical document understanding by extracting important information from documents such as discharge summaries, lab reports, and prescriptions.

To assist patients in monitoring their recovery progress by allowing them to record daily health indicators such as symptoms, temperature, weight, and overall well-being.

To identify potential health risks early by analyzing patient inputs and medical information to detect abnormal patterns during recovery.

To provide personalized recovery guidance including dietary suggestions, hydration reminders, and recovery recommendations based on patient condition.

To improve patient awareness and engagement by presenting medical information in a clear and easy-to-understand format.

To support better communication between patients and healthcare providers by organizing recovery data and health summaries.

3. Key Features of the System

The proposed application includes several important features designed to improve the post-surgery recovery experience.

1. Medical Document Analysis

The system allows users to upload medical documents such as discharge summaries, laboratory reports, prescriptions, and radiology reports. The application analyzes the documents and extracts key medical information such as diagnoses, procedures, medications, and important test values.

2. Simplified Medical Information

The system converts complex medical terminology into simple explanations that patients can easily understand. This helps patients become more aware of their medical condition and recovery requirements.

3. Daily Health Monitoring

Patients can record daily health indicators such as temperature, weight, symptoms, and general recovery status. This information helps track the patient's recovery progress over time.

4. Risk Detection and Alerts

By analyzing health indicators and medical information, the system can identify abnormal patterns that may indicate potential complications and provide early warnings.

5. Personalized Recovery Guidance

The system provides recommendations related to diet, hydration, medication reminders, and recovery activities based on the patient’s condition.

6. Recovery Progress Visualization

The application presents recovery data using charts and summaries, allowing patients to easily monitor improvements or changes in their health condition.

4. Scope of the System

The scope of the proposed system focuses on supporting patients during the recovery phase after surgery by providing digital assistance for monitoring and understanding medical information.

The system is designed to analyze medical documents and help patients interpret important medical details that are often difficult to understand. It also enables patients to track their recovery progress through daily health data inputs and provides insights that help them identify potential health concerns.

The application primarily targets patients recovering at home after surgical procedures, especially those who require continuous observation during the recovery period. It can also be useful for caregivers who assist patients during recovery.

While the system provides helpful guidance and insights, it is not intended to replace professional medical advice. Instead, it acts as a supportive tool that enhances patient awareness and encourages timely consultation with healthcare professionals when necessary.

The system can be further expanded in the future to integrate additional health monitoring features, improved medical data analysis capabilities, and better communication support between patients and healthcare providers.
